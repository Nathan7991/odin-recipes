Vegetable Risotto

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 40 mins

Total Time: 1 hr

Servings: 4

Description

Vegetable Risotto is a classic, Vegetarian dish enjoyed by various Hylians. Due to this dish's sweetness, it has a mild flavor. The dish is made by cooking Hylian Rice, Goat Butter, Rock Salt, and either a Fortified Pumpkin or any Carrot together in a cooking pot of your choice. It is mainly made by using ingredients with contradicting status effects. You can eat them to heal some Hearts to your Life Gauge.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of Hylian Rice
  • Goat Butter
  • Rock Salt
  • Chopped Fortified Pumpkin or any Carrot
  • Miso Dashi Stock
  • x2 4 cups of water

Steps

  1. Begin by making the miso dashi stock. Combine the 4 cups of water, chopped pumpkin or carrot, and rock salt in a large pot.
  2. Mix the ingredients in the pot.
  3. Place the broth in the pot, introducing it over a low heat slowly.
  4. In a small pan, heat the goat butter to a melt
  5. Add in 4 cups of water and Hylian Rice to cook. Cover the Pan.
  6. After the rice is of a fluffy text, add the combined ingredients from steps 1 and 2 onto the pan.
