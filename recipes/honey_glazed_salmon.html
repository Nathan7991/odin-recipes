Honey Glazed Salmon

Prep Time: 30 mins

Cook Time: 40 mins

Total Time: 1 hr and 10 mins

Servings: 4

Description

Made exclusively with the finest salmon curated throughout Hyrule, Honey Glazed Salmon has been a fan favorite of Hylian familes throughout the ages. With a sweet kick of honey soy sauce, there aren't many who can resist the flavor themselves; not even Ganondorf himself.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb salmon, skin on and cut into 3 portions
  • 1/2 cup of honey
  • 1 tbsp white miso
  • 2 tbsp sake
  • 1/4 cup of rice wine
  • x2 4 cups of water

Steps

  1. Combine the honey, miso, sake, rice wine, and water into a bowl. Let this marinate in the refrigerator along with your salmon for 30 minutes
  2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit. Prepare a baking tray with aluminum foil and nonstick spray. Afterwhich, place the salmon on the foil to bake for approximately 20 minutes.
  3. Serve when ready.
